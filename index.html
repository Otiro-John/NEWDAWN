How We Began

Born From a Burden, Formed by a Call

New Dawn Fellowship began with a growing burden we could not ignore. We encountered many people who genuinely desired God but felt disconnected from church, community, and clarity in their faith. They carried questions, wounds, and hunger for truth, yet often felt unseen or unsure where they belonged. This burden drove us to prayer, where it became clear that God was responding to what He was seeing in the hearts of His people.

A place where Scripture is taught clearly, fellowship is genuine, and people are guided into a steady and healthy walk with God. Just as God sent Moses to lead His people out, we believe New Dawn Fellowship exists to help people come out of isolation and into healing, belonging, and purpose.

What We Believe
What We Did First

Before the Spotlight, There Was A Burden

We Started With Teaching

Before programs or expansion, we committed ourselves to teaching Scripture clearly and practically. Our first work was creating a space where people could understand God’s Word without pressure, confusion, or religious complexity. We focused on helping people build a strong foundation of faith rooted in truth, clarity, and daily application.

We Built Fellowship Before Structure

Alongside teaching, we intentionally created room for fellowship. We gathered people consistently, prayed together, listened to one another, and built relationships that felt safe and genuine. Before systems or visibility, we chose to build trust, belonging, and community—believing that spiritual growth happens best in shared life, not isolation.
